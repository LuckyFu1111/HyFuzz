================================================================================
KEY FINDINGS FOR THESIS
================================================================================

1. MODBUS VALIDITY:
   - High PSR (87.1%) indicates good protocol compliance
   - Moderate EXR (10.7%) shows effective exception handling
   - Low latency (1.4ms) demonstrates efficiency

2. MODBUS STATE COVERAGE:
   - 264 unique states discovered in 500 trials
   - Coverage saturation around trial 250
   - Good exploration of FC×Address space

3. MODBUS FUZZING:
   - Consistent throughput: 666.6 exec/s (±16.2)
   - High crash discovery: 124 unique crashes (±10.4)
   - Low variance across trials indicates stability

4. CoAP COHERENCE:
   - Excellent ACK ratio (94.7%) and Token coherence (99.1%)
   - DTLS has minimal impact on coherence (<0.5%)
   - High success rate (75.3% 2xx responses)

5. CoAP MILESTONES:
   - Strong Observe support: 48 registrations, 42 cycles
   - Good Blockwise performance: 12 Block1, 15 Block2 completions
   - Full SZX diversity explored (7 different sizes)
   - DTLS reduces milestone achievement by ~6-8%

6. BASELINE COMPARISONS:
   - HyFuzz outperforms all baselines in crash discovery
   - 76.2% more crashes than AFL on Modbus
   - 84.2% more crashes than AFL on CoAP
   - Best overall balance of throughput and effectiveness

7. DTLS IMPACT:
   - 15.3% throughput overhead (acceptable)
   - Minimal impact on coherence and correctness
   - Proportional reduction in milestone achievement
